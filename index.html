About UnReel

Problem Statement

Students and young adults often recognize that excessive social media harms focus, mood, and self-esteem, but awareness alone rarely leads to sustained behavior change.

Proposed Solution

UnReel pairs real-time usage visibility with social accountability so users can make mindful, self-directed decisions about scrolling before it becomes automatic.

Primary Users

College students and early-career adults who want better digital balance without removing social media from their daily lives.

How UnReel differs from digital detox apps

Many digital detox tools rely on hard restrictions, guilt, or moral messaging. UnReel centers on voluntary change, social motivation, and contextual self-awareness—helping users build habits they can actually maintain.

Process

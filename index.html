Why this problem matters

Young adults spend over 3 hours a day on social media. This stage of life is when habits become deeply ingrained, so excessive scrolling can have long-term impact on self-image, social skills, and attention.

What makes UnReel novel

Existing tools rely on lockouts and self-control alone. UnReel shifts behavior change into a social context with shared reflection and friendly competition.

Closest competitor

OneSec is the closest product: it inserts delays before opening distracting apps. While useful for impulse interruption, delays can become easy to ignore. UnReel addresses sustained motivation through accountability with friends.

The Story

Why we built Unreel

The Problem

Willpower alone doesn't work

Young adults spend over 3 hours daily on social media. This is a time when the brain is most malleable and habits stick for life. Existing tools like screen-time blockers rely entirely on individual self-control. Our needfinding revealed the real gap isn't awareness. People know they scroll too much. The gap is motivation. Nobody feels accountable to anyone when scrolling alone at 1am.

The Solution

Gamify stopping the scroll

Unreel shifts screen-time reduction into a game with friends. Set personal goals, send challenges to friends, and compete on a live leaderboard. The thing missing from every screen-time app is the people who actually know you. Unlike OneSec which interrupts the impulse with a delay you can wait out, Unreel targets the underlying motivation through shared reflection and friendly competition that's fundamentally harder to ignore.
